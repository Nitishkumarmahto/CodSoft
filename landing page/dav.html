About

Welcome to the A B C SCHOOL,Dholakpur. This school was established on 9thApril, 2007 for providing better quality education to specially for Rural and Remote Areas students.The school provides fully green campus thats spread over approx 4 acres of land. It is just 5 km. away from the NH-2 Atka Chowk, Bagodar(GIRIDIH). The school morning commences with the chanting of Vedic Mantras during Assembly . The school takes care of the holistic evolvement of the students by providing them plenty of opportunities for participating in co-curricular activities. We also Provides better convenient through transport facilities to reaching the school.
